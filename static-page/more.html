Lisbon is renowned for the azulejos, the blue and white tiles that adorn many of the older buildings. Tiled houses often have ornate doors, some wrought iron and others of beautifully carved wood. Lisbon has been the setting-off point for many historical voyages, including those of Vasco De Gama, and the area of Belém has a tower to commemorate the trip, as well as the remarkable Jerónimos Monastery.
